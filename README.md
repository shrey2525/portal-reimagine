## Core Features

### 1. Multi-Touch Portal Creation
Create portals using intuitive mobile gestures:
- **Pinch**: Scale portals (0.5m - 5m)
- **Rotate**: Orient portals in 3D space
- **Swipe**: Paint portal boundaries
- **Double-tap**: Activate portals

Each gesture triggers custom haptic feedback for tactile immersion.

### 2. Gyroscope Scanner
Your phone becomes a reality scanner:
- **Tilt**: Reveal hidden energy signatures
- **Shake**: Disperse energy clouds
- **Orientation Puzzles**: Solve challenges requiring precise device positioning

25+ energy signatures scattered across 5 unique zones.

### 3. Collaborative Touch Mechanics
Team up for synchronized challenges:
- **Simultaneous Touch**: Multiple players touch screens together
- **Energy Relay**: Pass orbs with perfect timing
- **Portal Networks**: Build interconnected portals

Synchronized haptic feedback creates shared moments of triumph.

### 4. Social Integration
Seamless Meta platform connectivity:
- **One-Tap Sharing**: Post to Instagram/Facebook with custom frames
- **QR Code Invites**: Instant world joining
- **Leaderboards**: Compete globally
- **Achievements**: 25+ mobile-specific challenges

### 5. Five Unique Zones
1. **Training Grounds**: Interactive tutorial with gesture training
2. **Crystal Caverns**: Gyroscope puzzles and multi-touch energy collection
3. **Sky Platforms**: Precision portal placement and collaboration
4. **Social Hub**: Leaderboards, portal gallery, photo opportunities
5. **Infinite Nexus**: Player-created portal networks
